| Column | Description | Possible Values / Unit |
|--------|-------------|------------------------|
| **Order_ID** | Unique identifier for each order. | Unique ID |
| **Distance_km** | Delivery distance from the restaurant to the customer. | Kilometers (km) |
| **Weather** | Weather conditions during the delivery. | Clear, Rainy, Snowy, Foggy, Windy |
| **Traffic_Level** | Traffic conditions at the time of delivery. | Low, Medium, High |
| **Time_of_Day** | Time period when the delivery took place. | Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night |
| **Vehicle_Type** | Type of vehicle used for the delivery. | Bike, Scooter, Car |
| **Preparation_Time_min** | Time required to prepare the order before dispatch. | Minutes (min) |
| **Courier_Experience_yrs** | Courier's delivery experience. | Years (yrs) |
| **Delivery_Time_min** | Total time taken to complete the delivery (**target variable**). | Minutes (min) |
